Cut Your Losses!

Learning to Prune Paths Early for Efficient Parallel Reasoning

Jiaxi Bi, Tongxu Luo, Zhengyang Tang, Benyou Wang

📄 Paper 💻 Code

TL;DR

STOP introduces a lightweight module that prunes unpromising reasoning paths early, reducing token usage while improving accuracy.

Motivation

Most reasoning paths are doomed from early stages. We aim to detect and prune them efficiently.

Method

We introduce a special token [STOP] that reads internal KV cache states and predicts prefix quality.

Results

On AIMO3 competition, STOP improves score from 39 to 43 (Silver-level).
